.5 lb lean ground beef
.5 lb ground Italian Sausage
1 package Barilla spaghetti
1 large container of Ragu original marinara sauce
salt and pepper to taste

1. Brown ground beef and sausage in large sauce pan, adding salt and pepper to preferred taste
2. Add sauce and let simmer for 5-10 minutes
3. Bring water to boil in large pot
4. Add spaghetti to water and reduce heat to medium-low
5. Cook spaghetti for 8-9 minutes or until desired softness
6. Remove spaghetti and strain
7. Add meat sauce to spaghetti and enjoy!
